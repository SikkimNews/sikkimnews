About Us

Sikkim News is an independent digital news platform dedicated to delivering accurate, timely, and community-focused news from every district of Sikkim.

Established in 2018, we have remained committed to keeping the people of Sikkim informed by covering local events, government announcements, education, tourism, business, culture, sports, public affairs, and inspiring stories that shape our communities.

Our mission is to provide reliable, unbiased, and responsible journalism that informs, connects, and empowers the people of Sikkim. We believe that access to trustworthy information strengthens communities, encourages transparency, and promotes informed public participation.

Beyond breaking news, Sikkim News aims to preserve and highlight the state's rich culture, traditions, achievements, and developmental progress. We are committed to giving a voice to local communities and bringing attention to issues that matter most to the people of Sikkim.

With a growing digital presence across social media platforms, we strive to make credible news accessible anytime and anywhere, ensuring our audience stays connected with the latest updates from across the state.
